Delete Your Swipe2Shop Account

Last updated: April 2026

You can request the deletion of your Swipe2Shop account and all associated data by following the steps below. We will process your request within 7 days.

How to request account deletion:
  1. Send an email to swipe2shop00@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address associated with your Swipe2Shop account
  4. We will confirm your request and delete your account within 7 days

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account details (username, email, password, phone, date of birth, gender)
  • Your swipe history (liked and disliked products)
  • Your personal style preference vector
  • Your wishlist
  • Your recommendation history
  • Your session data

What data is retained

After account deletion, no personally identifiable data is retained. Anonymised, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for service improvement purposes.

Partial data deletion (optional)

If you would like to delete only specific data — such as your swipe history or wishlist — without deleting your entire account, you can also request this by emailing swipe2shop00@gmail.com with details of what you would like removed.

Contact

For any questions about data deletion, contact us at swipe2shop00@gmail.com.
